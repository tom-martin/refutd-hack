What is Refugees United?

We are an organisation that offers a safe, secure and anonymous way to find family and friends that you have lost contact with. This service is FREE of charge.

Using our search tool, you are able to look for missing friends and family, even though you are far from home. At the same time you make it possible for them to find you.
